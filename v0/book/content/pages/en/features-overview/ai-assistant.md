# AI Assistant

Hubleto's built-in **AI Assistant** supercharges your workflow by giving you a smart helper directly inside your CRM and ERP.

## Key Features

1. **Two Modes:**
   - **User Mode:** Acts as your personal helper. Ask it to summarize data, draft emails, or answer general questions.
   - **Developer Mode:** For technical staff to write code, debug issues, and manage the Hubleto framework from the chat.
2. **Context Sensitivity:** Administrators can control exactly how much background information about a record is automatically sent to the AI.
3. **Magic Wand Tool:** Found across Hubleto forms, the magic wand lets you generate context-aware content instantly with a single click.
