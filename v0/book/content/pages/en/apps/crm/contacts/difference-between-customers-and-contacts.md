# Difference between a customer and a contact

At first, the difference between a customer and a contact can seem unclear. In Hubleto CRM, they represent two different types of records.

## Key distinction

**Customer**

A customer is an entity you cooperate with. It can be:

* an individual person, or
* a company/organization.

**Contact**

A contact is a record of a specific person (for example, an employee, manager, or assistant) who is linked to a customer.

## How to think about it in practice

In many cases, a contact is related to a customer, but these lists are not the same:

* One customer can have multiple contacts.
* Some contacts may not be active customers yet (for example, people you communicated with during early discussions).
* Your contacts list is usually broader than your customers list because it stores all relevant people you have interacted with.

## Summary

| Record type | Meaning |
| ----------- | ------- |
| Customer | A person or company your business cooperates with. |
| Contact | A specific individual linked to a customer or business communication. |
